Welcome to Our Church Family

We are RCISA Johannesburg North, a vibrant branch of the Reformed Church International South Africa, proudly part of the Reformed Church in Zimbabwe (RCZ) family.

Founded on a rich heritage dating back to September 9, 1891, we continue the legacy of faith, worship, and service that has defined the RCZ for over 130 years.

Whether you're looking for a church home, seeking spiritual growth, or want to serve your community, we welcome you with open arms.
